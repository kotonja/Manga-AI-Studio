## What To Test

- Open the dashboard and onboarding.
- Run the Founder Demo.
- Create a demo manga project.
- Open Story Room, Character Lab, Style Lab, Page Studio, QA Room, and Publishing Room.
- Try an export download.
- Submit feedback when anything is confusing, broken, slow, or surprising.

## What Not To Upload

- No private personal documents.
- No confidential work files.
- No copyrighted manga pages, franchise art, or images you do not have rights to use.
- No images of minors or sensitive personal data.
- No production/commercial assets.

## How To Report Bugs

Use the in-app feedback button when possible. Include:

- What you were trying to do.
- What happened.
- What you expected.
- The page/project/panel you were on.
- Whether the bug happened once or repeats.

If the app is unavailable, send the same information to `replace-with-support-contact`.
